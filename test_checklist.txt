ECOMMBX Compliance Checklist

SECTION 1: Company Info
1. Legal Name: The full legal name of the company must be visible.
2. Reg Number: Company registration number must be present.
3. Address: Registered legal address must be shown.

SECTION 2: Policies
4. Privacy Policy: Must have a privacy policy link.
5. Refund Policy: Must describe refund conditions.
6. Terms: Terms and Conditions must be present.
